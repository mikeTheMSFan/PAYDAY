PAY DAY: A Coding Project

PAY DAY is a JavaScript exercise using loops. PAY DAY is a JavaScript exercise using loops. The app parses your pay and day values which is a number between 1 and 10. If your pay value is divisible by a number between 1 and, 100 the word PAY will appear! On the other hand, if your day value is divisible by this set, DAY will appear. If PAYDAY appears, both of your picks are divisible by the number in question. Good luck!

Instructions

Enter a 'PAY' and 'DAY' value (between 1 and 10) and the numbers will appear.
